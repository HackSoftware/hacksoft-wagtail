What we do

Projects from scratch

We provide an end-to-end solutions for your business.
To achieve best results, to ensure maximum flexibility for your project and to deliver new features for You we use the Scrum software development methodology which gives us the opportunity to focus on 10-day sprints and keep your project agile.

Start-up’s kit

Most of the time Start-ups are on a tight schedule and they experience the need outsourers to create prototypes as fast as possible.
We recognize that need and keep a close communication with our clients and make daily priority updates of the project’s progress.

Exciting existing projects

Your software needs new features or bug fixing? We are here to help!
We provide an expert advisory, support and enhacing services for your existing projects.
We rely on the Kanban Method to ensure the just-in-time delivery of the results you persue.

The Process

Book a meeting

A member of your company fills up our form with information about your company and your project.

Discussion

We meet to discuss your company’s needs (in person or via skype).
We consult you on your project and provide some advices and suggestions on why some things may work and why others may not work.

Planning

We develop a strategy and plan for your project.

Development

Our developers follow the plan and ensure their work fits the needs and goals of your project.
The thechonologies we use for the development process you can find here.

Test and Fix

The phase where we test the product and fix the bugs to ensure you receive the best results.

Deployment

When we receive the final approval from you we deploy our work and bring the project online.
